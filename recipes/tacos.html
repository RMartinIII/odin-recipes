Tacos

Recipe for traditional beef tacos perfect for your taco Tuesday

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Whole Tomatoes
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Mix 1/2 lb of ground beef with spices and diced onion while heating olive oil in pan
  2. Let ground beef simmer in pan then start breaking up in small intervals to avoid greying meat
  3. Dice tomatoes and shred lettuce
  4. Place meat in tortilla shell followed by tomatoes, lettuce and shredded cheese
  5. Enjoy
